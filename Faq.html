Frequently Asked Questions
+
You can contact me through the 'Contact Form' on my website or via text/phone call.
Once we connect, I can answer any questions you may have, discuss your design ideas and go over other details. From there, we'll establish a quote and estimated start date. To confirm your booking, I require a non-refundable security deposit of $75 CAD, which will be deducted from the total amount quoted.
+
The baseline for painting a mask/helmet 1 solid color is $250 CAD. With added colours and designs the average cost ranges between $400-$600.
Prices vary with added colours, design and complexity—to provide an accurate cost, please contact me with your interests and ideas.
