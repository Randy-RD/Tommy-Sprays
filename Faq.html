Frequently Asked Questions
+
You can contact me through the 'Contact Form' on my website or via text/phone call.
Once we connect, I can answer any questions you may have, discuss your design ideas and go over other details about the process.
+
Most custom goalie masks I take on start at $1,000 CAD. Final pricing depends on the concept, level of detail, and revisions.
I am currently focused on more detailed, full-custom designs (no replicas). All masks are outsourced to a local body-shop and coated with High Impact Clear Coat which is factored into the base price.
+
While I have experience painting a variety of things, I am currently putting a heavier focus on goalie masks; though if you do have a project that is not a goalie mask, I still encourage you to reach out to me about your project! *Note I can not paint surfaces made of plastic (polyehtylene), and specific goalie masks such as the Bauer 930 or CCM Axis F5.
